# Financial Analysis Dashboard (Tableau)

## Overview
This project presents an interactive **Financial Analysis Dashboard** built using **Tableau**, designed to analyze sales, profit, and discount performance across multiple countries, products, customer segments, and time periods. The dashboard helps stakeholders quickly identify profitability drivers and areas requiring optimization.

## Dataset
(https://github.com/shubhlovesdata/Unified_Mentor_Internship_Projects/blob/f61b76cfa156d6662a74afdfc3202b0211734fb4/Finance%20Report%20Analysis/financial_data.xlsx)

## Key KPIs
- **Total Sales:** $118.7M  
- **Total Profit:** $17.67M  
- **Profit Margin:** 14.88%  
- **Average Discount:** $13.15K
  
## Dashboard Features
- **Sales & Profit Trend by Time:** Year-wise comparison highlighting strong growth from 2013 to 2014  
- **Sales by Country (Map & Table):** Geographic performance with profit and sales comparison  
- **Profit by Product (Treemap):** Identifies high-performing products such as *Paseo* and *VTT*  
- **Sales by Product & Discount Band (Heatmap):** Shows the impact of discount levels on product sales  
- **Segment Performance (Stacked Bar):** Compares sales contribution across customer segments and discount bands  

## Insights
- Government and Small Business segments contribute the highest sales  
- Products with **medium to high discounts** drive volume but can impact margins  
- Paseo is the most profitable product across markets  
- Strong overall growth observed in 2014 compared to 2013  

## Filters & Interactivity
- Country  
- Product  
- Year  
- Segment  
- Discount Band  

All visuals are fully interactive and update dynamically based on filter selections.

## Tools Used
- **Tableau** – Dashboard creation & visualization  
- **Excel** – Data source and preprocessing
